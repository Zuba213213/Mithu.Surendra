Where You Are.

I didn't arrive here with answers. I arrived here with damage. With shame I couldn't name, with pain I kept performing around, with a version of myself I'd built just to survive — and couldn't figure out how to let go of.

"The wound was never the problem. Not knowing how to walk through it — that was."

Not a shortcut. Not a quick fix. A real path — walked together, in the dark, toward the light that's already inside you.
