And the moment you see it, you can shift it, love it, heal it. It becomes a powerful part of your personality. Instead of having the pain playing in the background, you face it. By facing it, you embrace it. And it becomes a source of power."

To anyone considering it: "Go for it. Face your shadows, face your fears. You will come out even more empowered, more joyful, more free."
