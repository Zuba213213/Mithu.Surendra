The story

I didn't find this path.
It found me.

There was a version of me that had everything looking fine on the outside — and something quietly collapsing inside. I was disconnected. From myself, from love, from the thing that makes life feel like it actually means something.

I kept performing life instead of living it. I didn't even know that's what I was doing. It took pain to wake me up. Real, inescapable, sit-in-it pain.

"The wound was never the problem. Not knowing how to walk through it — that was."

What I found on the other side wasn't a fixed version of me. It was presence. Aliveness. Myself. That's what I'm here to help you find — not a shortcut, not a script. A real path, walked together.
